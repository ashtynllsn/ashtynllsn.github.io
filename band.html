More About Band

While I was definetly involved in many differnet things when I went to high school the thing that I spent the most time doing was band. Being in band helped develop my confidence in myself and teamwork skills. Not to mention I left with the pretty cool skill of being able to play the trumpet. I was in band for a total of eight years and loved all of it. I don't know if I can confidently say that I would have enjoyed high school as much as I did if it weren't for band.
